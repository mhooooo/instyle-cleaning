# InStyle Cleaning — Gainesville Zone Map

## Zone Definitions

### NW — Northwest Gainesville
**Boundaries**: West of NW 34th St, North of University Ave
**Key Areas**: Millhopper area, Haile Plantation, Turkey Creek, Jonesville
**Character**: Upscale suburban homes, families, established neighborhoods
**Typical Jobs**: Large 3-4BR homes, recurring weekly/biweekly
**Average Job Value**: $200+ (larger homes)
**Landmarks**: Devil's Millhopper State Park, Westgate Lanes, Publix at Millhopper

### NE — Northeast Gainesville
**Boundaries**: East of NW 34th St / Main St, North of University Ave
**Key Areas**: Eastside, Hawthorne Rd corridor, Gainesville Regional Airport area
**Character**: Mixed — some older homes, newer developments, diverse
**Typical Jobs**: Mix of standard and deep cleans
**Average Job Value**: $150–$180
**Landmarks**: Gainesville Regional Airport, Fred Bear Museum, Eastside High

### SW — Southwest Gainesville (UF Corridor)
**Boundaries**: West of Main St, South of University Ave
**Key Areas**: Archer Rd, Tower Rd, SW 20th Ave, UF campus perimeter, Butler Plaza
**Character**: Dense student apartments, complexes, young professionals
**Typical Jobs**: Move-out cleans (May/Aug), apartment standards
**Average Job Value**: $150–$200 (volume play)
**Peak Season**: May (move-out) and August (move-in) — demand triples
**Landmarks**: UF Ben Hill Griffin Stadium, Shands Hospital, Butler Plaza, Oaks Mall

### SE — Southeast Gainesville
**Boundaries**: East of Main St, South of University Ave
**Key Areas**: Williston Rd, SE industrial/commercial corridor, Waldo Rd south
**Character**: Commercial, industrial, some residential
**Typical Jobs**: Commercial cleaning (offices, warehouses)
**Average Job Value**: $300+ (commercial)
**Landmarks**: Gainesville Raceway, SE industrial parks

### DT — Downtown
**Boundaries**: University Ave corridor from 13th St to 6th St, Main St corridor
**Key Areas**: Downtown core, Midtown, Innovation District
**Character**: Mixed commercial/residential, restaurants, offices, condos
**Typical Jobs**: Small commercial, restaurant prep, condo/loft cleans
**Average Job Value**: $175
**Landmarks**: Bo Diddley Plaza, Hippodrome Theatre, Sun Center

**Notes:**
- Intra-zone travel averages 5 minutes (jobs are clustered)
- I-75 access from NW and SW zones helps with cross-town trips
- University Ave / Archer Rd congestion adds 5-10 min during UF class change (10am, 12pm, 2pm)
- Game day Saturdays (Fall): avoid DT and SW zones during 2 hours before/after kickoff

## Zone Optimization Strategy

1. **Morning**: Start with the farthest zone from base, work inward
2. **Cluster**: Never schedule a NW job between two SE jobs — the drive kills efficiency
3. **Peak hours**: Avoid University Ave crossings between 11am-1pm
4. **Rainy days**: Add 5 min buffer to all drive times (Florida afternoon storms)
5. **Game days**: Pre-schedule SW and DT jobs before noon; they're inaccessible by 2pm
