# InStyle Cleaning — Pricing Guide

## Residential Pricing

### Standard Clean ($120–$250)
The bread-and-butter service. Includes all living areas, kitchen, bathrooms.

**Formula**: `base ($80) + (rooms × $20) + (sqft × $0.03)`

| Home Size | Rooms | Sqft | Calculated | Rounded |
|-----------|-------|------|-----------|---------|
| Studio/1BR | 2 | 600 | $138 | $140 |
| 2BR Apt | 3 | 1000 | $170 | $170 |
| 3BR House | 5 | 1500 | $225 | $225 |
| 4BR House | 6 | 2200 | $266 | $250* |

*Capped at $250 for standard clean. Larger homes quoted individually.

**Includes:**
- Dusting all surfaces
- Vacuuming/mopping all floors
- Kitchen: counters, sink, appliance exteriors, stove top
- Bathrooms: toilet, shower/tub, sink, mirror, floor
- Beds made, trash emptied

### Deep Clean ($250–$500)
Thorough cleaning for first-time clients or seasonal refreshes. Everything in standard plus baseboards, inside cabinets, light fixtures, behind appliances.

**Formula**: `base ($150) + (rooms × $35) + (sqft × $0.06)`

| Home Size | Rooms | Sqft | Calculated | Rounded |
|-----------|-------|------|-----------|---------|
| Studio/1BR | 2 | 600 | $256 | $260 |
| 2BR Apt | 3 | 1000 | $315 | $315 |
| 3BR House | 5 | 1500 | $415 | $415 |
| 4BR House | 6 | 2200 | $492 | $490 |
