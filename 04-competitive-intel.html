🧠 SWOT Analysis

💪 Strengths

  • Marriott/hospitality-trained — quality signal to customers
  • Post-construction niche — less competition, higher margins
  • Strong Instagram game — consistent Reels content
  • Multi-city ambition (GNV + Orlando + Tampa)
  • "Luxury at affordable prices" positioning hits the sweet spot

⚠️ Weaknesses

  • Near-zero Google reviews — invisible in local search
  • Wix website — poor SEO, slow, unprofessional at scale
  • No Google Business Profile visible
  • Young brand (est. 2020) vs. decades-old competitors
  • Small team limits capacity for multi-city service

🎯 Opportunities

  • UF student turnover — massive seasonal demand (May, Aug, Dec)
  • Housing market growing 8.3% YoY — more post-construction jobs
  • Airbnb/short-term rental cleaning market expanding
  • Google Business optimization — easy win, immediate visibility
  • Apartment complex partnerships for turnover contracts

⚡ Threats

  • Established competitors with 100+ reviews and loyal bases
  • National franchises (JAN-PRO, Anago) with brand muscle
  • Low median income ($46K) — extreme price sensitivity
  • Premature expansion risk — Gainesville not yet dominated
  • Labor market competition for cleaning staff
