Brand Voice Guide

Consistent messaging guidelines so every piece of content sounds unmistakably InStyle.

🎯 Professional but Warm

We're experts, not robots. Competent and caring.

✅ "We treat every home like our own"
✅ "Our team is fully trained and insured"
❌ "Our operatives execute cleaning protocols"
❌ "Hey bestie!! We're SO obsessed with cleaning!!"

💪 Confident, Not Arrogant

We know we're good. We let the work speak.

✅ "This is what InStyle standard looks like"
✅ "We clean what others forget"
❌ "We're the BEST cleaning company EVER"
❌ "Nobody else even comes close"

🏠 Local & Relatable

We're your neighbor, not a franchise.

✅ "Serving Gainesville families since 2020"
✅ "We know what Gainesville landlords check"
❌ "Our nationwide operations..."
❌ Generic content with no local mentions

✨ Detail-Oriented

Show the thoroughness. Be specific.

✅ "Every baseboard, every vent, every light switch"
✅ "We do a walkthrough checklist before we leave"
❌ "We clean really well"
❌ "Your home will be nice and clean"

🤝 Trustworthy

Trust is everything when you let people into your home.

✅ "Insured, trained, and background-checked"
✅ "Same trusted team every visit"
❌ Overpromise ("We guarantee perfection!")
❌ Ignore concerns or negative feedback

📣 Action-Oriented CTAs

Always tell people what to do next.

✅ "Book in 60 seconds" / "DM us CLEAN"
✅ "Get a free estimate" / "Link in bio"
❌ End posts without any CTA
❌ "Contact us maybe if you want"
