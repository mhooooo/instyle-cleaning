Usage Guidelines

Do's and don'ts for maintaining brand consistency

✔ Do

Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "I" in InStyle

✘ Don't

Never stretch, rotate, or distort the logo. Always scale proportionally.

✔ Do

Use Navy + Gold as the primary combination. White is always acceptable as a background.

✘ Don't

Never place the logo on busy, patterned, or low-contrast backgrounds.

✔ Do

Use Playfair Display for headlines and Inter for body text. Maintain the typographic hierarchy.

✘ Don't

Never substitute fonts. If Playfair is unavailable, use Georgia as fallback. Never use Comic Sans.

✔ Do

Include sparkle elements in marketing materials — they're part of the brand identity representing cleanliness.

✘ Don't

Never change the gold accent color to yellow, orange, or brown. The specific gold (#C9A84C) is the brand signature.
